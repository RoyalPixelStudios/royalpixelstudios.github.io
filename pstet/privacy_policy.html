Privacy Policy

PSTET Exam Preparation — Last Updated: 20 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PSTET Exam Preparation, developed and published by Royal Pixel Studios, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

1. Overview

PSTET Exam Preparation is an educational app designed to help candidates prepare for the Punjab State Teacher Eligibility Test (PSTET). We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring transparency in how data is handled.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, email address, or physical location.

2.2 Local Test Data

Your test attempts, scores, and history are stored locally on your device using secure storage. This information:

  • Is not uploaded to our servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Can be deleted by clearing app data or uninstalling the app

2.3 Advertising & Advertising ID

The app displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • App interaction data

This information is used for serving and measuring advertisements. We do not link advertising identifiers to personal identity.

2.4 Consent (EEA / GDPR)

Users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland may be presented with a consent form for personalized ads. Users can:

  • Accept personalized ads
  • Choose non-personalized ads
  • Modify consent anytime from the app’s Privacy Settings

2.5 In-App Purchases

The app offers a non-consumable purchase to remove advertisements permanently. All purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing.

  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • Purchase validation is handled by Google Play
  • Premium status may be restored automatically when reinstalling the app

2.6 Diagnostic & Performance Data

We may use Google services (such as Crash Reporting tools) to collect anonymized technical data including:

  • Crash logs
  • Device model and OS version
  • App performance metrics

This data is used solely to improve app stability and performance.

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve app functionality and reliability
  • Fix crashes and bugs
  • Serve relevant advertisements
  • Enable premium purchase functionality

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. Limited data may be processed by:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (Purchases)
  • Google diagnostic services

5. Data Security

  • No personal accounts are required
  • No personal data is stored on our servers
  • All payments are processed securely by Google Play

6. Data Retention

Local test data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app storage.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for exam aspirants and students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your data, including the right to withdraw consent for personalized ads.

9. No Government Affiliation

This app is not affiliated with any government organization. It is an independent educational tool created for exam preparation purposes only.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
